FAQs for Soil Analytics

How does it work?

Our product functions by utilizing two sensors: the DHT22 Temperature and Humidity Sensor and the MQ135 Air Quality Sensor. These sensors collect data from the crop environment, including temperature, humidity, and air quality. This information is then processed to determine the appropriate actions to take. Commands are sent to various systems such as the drip irrigation and heater systems based on the analyzed data. Additionally, the data collected is displayed in real-time on the accompanying mobile application, providing users with insights into the crop conditions.

Is it easy to use?

Yes, our product is designed to be user-friendly. The data collected from the sensors is presented in a clear and understandable format on the mobile application, allowing users to easily monitor the crop conditions.

What crops can it handle?

From wheat to watermelons, our device is designed to cater to a wide range of crops, ensuring versatility and efficiency.

Does it save time?

Yes, our prototype can save time for farmers and growers. By automating tasks such as monitoring environmental conditions, controlling irrigation, and adjusting heating systems based on real-time data, it reduces the need for manual intervention.
